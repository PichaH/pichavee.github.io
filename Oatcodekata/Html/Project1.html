Choi Jin-ri (Sulli)

The girl who made a shine world

Here's a time line of Sulli's Life:

  • 1994 - Born in Busan,South Korea.
  • 2005 - Joined SM Entertainment and debuted as a child actress at age 11 in the SBS drama Ballad of Seodong.
  • 2009 - Debuted in the girl group f(x) with the digital single "La Cha Ta".
  • 2013 - Sulli became the face of makeup brand Etude House along with fellow f(x) member Krystal.
  • 2014 - Sulli appeared in hit films, The Pirate and Fashion King.
  • On August 7,2015 - SM announced that Sulli had left f(x) to focus on her acting career.
  • 2018 - Sulli was a featured singer in DEAN's single "Dayfly".
  • On June,2019 - Debuted as a soloist with the digital single album "Goblin".
  • On October 14,2019 - Sulli was found dead at her home in Seongnam, Gyeonggi-do by her manager who was unable to reach her the day before.
